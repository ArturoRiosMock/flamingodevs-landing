## How to Start Without Spending a Fortune

You don't need a team of data scientists or a million-dollar budget. Here are practical steps:

1. **Identify repetitive tasks** - What does your team do over and over?
2. **Choose a simple tool** - There are no-code solutions available
3. **Start small** - One process at a time
4. **Measure results** - What's not measured can't be improved
